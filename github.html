In the fall of 2020, as the pandemic persisted, the Columbia University School of Engineering offered its coding boot camp virtually for the first time. It's a 6-month program, with 3-hour classes four times per week. I did it while working full time. On this page I've included some of the apps I created for boot camp.

If you're thinking about enrolling in a boot camp to advance your career, do it! It's the best educational investment I've ever made.
